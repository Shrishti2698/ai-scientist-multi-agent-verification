# Phase 7: Thesis and Demo Preparation

## Goal

Convert the implemented system and experimental results into a presentation-ready MTech research project.

## Deliverables

- final architecture diagram,
- methodology chapter outline,
- experiment tables,
- failure analysis examples,
- and a minimal demo flow.

## Demo Strategy

The demo should show:

1. a research question,
2. retrieved papers,
3. extracted claims,
4. verification results with evidence,
5. critique notes,
6. and the final report.

## Thesis Angle

The presentation should emphasize:

- why single-pass LLM answers are risky,
- why standard RAG is still insufficient,
- how orchestration changes the reasoning process,
- and what the experiments show about its benefits and limits.
