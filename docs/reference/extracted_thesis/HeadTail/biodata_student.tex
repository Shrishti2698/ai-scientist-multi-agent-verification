{\LARGE U}rmila was born in the village of Amodidih, Mahasamund, Chhattisgarh, India, on 22$^{\mathrm{nd}}$ May 1996. She completed her entire schooling in her hometown. After finishing school, she enrolled at Pt. Ravishankar Shukla University to pursue her undergraduate studies in Physics, Computer, and Mathematics, followed by postgraduate studies in Information Technology.

Driven by a deep passion for research and inspired by her brother's dream, she decided to pursue a Ph.D. To achieve this goal, she qualified both the UGC-NET and GATE examinations. Based on her performance, she secured admission to the Master of Technology program in Computer Science and Engineering with a specialization in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence at Dr. Shyama Prasad Mukherjee International Institute of Information Technology, Naya Raipur, Chhattisgarh (IIIT-NR).

At IIIT-NR, Urmila worked under the guidance of Dr. Avantika Singh. During this time, she explored and learned many essential skills that laid the foundation for her research journey. Her efforts culminated in the publication of her first research paper, which was presented at the ICPRAM Conference in Portugal in 2025. Her work was recognized with the Best Poster Award, marking a promising start to her academic career. This achievement has further fueled her enthusiasm to explore and grow in the field of research.

Beyond her academic pursuits, Urmila finds joy in spending time with friends, which contributes positively to her personal happiness and self-growth.
