{\Large{\textbf{Preface}}}\\
In this chapter, we present an introduction to remote photoplethysmography (rPPG) signals. We discuss what rPPG is, the reasons for choosing it as the focus of our study, the devices used to capture rPPG signals, and the various application areas that inspired this research particularly its potential in stress detection and biometric identification.\\
