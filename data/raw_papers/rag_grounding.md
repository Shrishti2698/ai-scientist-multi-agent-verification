Title: Retrieval-Augmented Language Models for Factual Grounding
Year: 2024
Authors: A. Verma, L. Chen
Topics: rag, hallucination, language models

Abstract: This study shows that retrieval-augmented generation improves factual grounding in large language models. The approach reduces unsupported statements when relevant documents are retrieved at inference time. However, performance depends heavily on retriever quality and corpus coverage.
