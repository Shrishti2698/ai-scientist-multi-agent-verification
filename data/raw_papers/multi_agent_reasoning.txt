Title: Multi-Agent Debate for Evidence-Sensitive Reasoning
Year: 2024
Authors: J. Malik, T. Howard
Topics: multi-agent, reasoning, evidence synthesis

Abstract: A multi-agent reasoning pipeline improves evidence coverage by assigning separate roles for retrieval, critique, and synthesis. The system achieves more consistent justifications than a single-agent baseline on knowledge-intensive tasks. The gains are limited when the evidence corpus is sparse.
