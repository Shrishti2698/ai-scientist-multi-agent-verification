\chapter{Reproducibility Artifacts}\label{appendix:artifacts}

\section{Purpose}

This appendix records the main files that support the PatchOps thesis results. It is intended as a navigation
aid for examiners and future maintainers rather than as a replacement for the full project repository. The
thesis follows the rule that per-phase \texttt{findings.md} files are the canonical source of quantitative
claims.
